[h1]Meet the Blobs[/h1]
King Reginald, a slightly-mad scientist with a paper crown, has been growing squishy little creatures in his lab. They got out. They're delightful. They will not stop bouncing.

[h1]How it plays[/h1]
Squish, jiggle, and rebound through contraption-stuffed levels. Every blob is full-physics — every bounce matters. Master momentum and you'll feel unstoppable.

[h1]Play with friends[/h1]
- Local multiplayer on a single screen
- Online play with full physics simulation on every client
- Host-authoritative outcomes so nothing ever feels unfair

[h1]Build your own[/h1]
A full level editor lets you design contraptions, triggers, and actions. Share your creations through Steam Workshop and play other players' levels with one click.

[h1]Why you'll love it[/h1]
Bouncy Blobs is short, silly, and surprisingly deep — the kind of game where five-minute "one more round" turns into two hours of laughter.
