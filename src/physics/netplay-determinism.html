netplay determinism validator

Two completely independent SoftBodyWorldHandle instances are constructed with the same seed and the same scripted input sequence. After every step we hash both worlds' state and compare. If the engine is deterministic across instances (which is the whole reason we ported it to integer math), the hashes must match exactly at every tick — that is the netplay guarantee in miniature.
