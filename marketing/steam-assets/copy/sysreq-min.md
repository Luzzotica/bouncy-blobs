<!-- Minimum system requirements. Windows is required; macOS/Linux optional. -->
[h2]Windows[/h2]
- OS: Windows 10 64-bit
- Processor: Intel Core i3 / AMD equivalent
- Memory: 4 GB RAM
- Graphics: GPU with WebGL 2.0 / DirectX 11 support
- Storage: 1 GB available space

[h2]macOS[/h2]
- OS: macOS 12 Monterey
- Processor: Apple Silicon or Intel Core i5
- Memory: 4 GB RAM
- Storage: 1 GB available space
